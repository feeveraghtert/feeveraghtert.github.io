Fee Veraghtert (°1998, Mol, Belgium) lives and works in Hasselt. Graduated in painting and printmaking at pxl-mad school of arts. Fee collects images from the everyday and translates them to canvas or paper. The works arise from a love for the medium and the possibilities that come with it. She focuses on the process of creating an abstract image. Through color, shape, line and texture she plays with the way a small sketch can be translated to a larger canvas. The results are often strongly monolithic forms, where simplicity and composition prevail. A glimpse of the origins of the works can be found in the titles.
